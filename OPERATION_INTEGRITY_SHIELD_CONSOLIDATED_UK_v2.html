Opening Note for This Release

This public release brings together the Integrity Shield findings and their supporting evidence reports in order to clarify a set of conspiracy claims already circulating in public discussion. The aim is not to create a new allegation, but to show how the available material separates a verified aviation accident from single-source claims, unresolved evidence gaps and rapidly mutating social-media narratives.

It has been prepared to support public confidence through evidentiary discipline: verified facts are stated as verified; plausible but unconfirmed details are preserved as unresolved; unsupported claims are not promoted into conclusions. In Starfleet terms, this is a sensor-fusion report: the bridge should not fire phasers because one panel blinks red; the command decision must wait for cross-checked telemetry.

Final Finding

Here is the consolidated assessment stated plainly:

Harmony Jets Flight 185 was an accident. Based on the uploaded assessment set, the strongest explanation is an electrical malfunction affecting a Dassault Falcon 50 over the Haymana area on 23 December 2025. Eight people died. The available assessment set does not support a shoot-down, electronic warfare intervention, foreign intelligence sabotage or a coordinated cover-up.

A later allegation concerning an Israeli-registered aircraft on the same apron remains unverified. It is relevant enough to preserve as an evidence gap, but not strong enough to change the primary finding.
